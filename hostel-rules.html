Hostel Rules & Regulations

  • Residents must return to the hostel by 9:00 PM.
  • Silence must be maintained in the hostel premises after 10:00 PM.
  • Keep your rooms clean and tidy at all times.
  • Visitors are only allowed between 4:00 PM to 7:00 PM and must be registered at the front desk.
  • Consumption of alcohol, drugs, or smoking is strictly prohibited.
  • Students must carry their ID cards at all times within the hostel.
  • Report any maintenance issues to the warden immediately.
  • Do not tamper with electrical installations or furniture.
